# SALES & MARKETING STRATEGY

**Goal**: Acquire first 100 paying customers in 12 months

## CUSTOMER AVATAR

### Primary: "Mid-Size Mike"
**Profile**:
- Age: 35-55
- Role: Owner/CEO of construction company
- Company size: $5M-$15M annual revenue, 10-30 employees
- Projects: 20-40 concurrent projects
- Current tools: Spreadsheets, QuickBooks, phone/email
- Pain: "Drowning in paperwork, losing track of projects"

**Buying Triggers**:
- Lost money on a project (didn't track costs)
- Client complained about lack of updates
- Failed inspection due to missed deadline
- Can't find a photo/document when needed
- Outgrew spreadsheets, can't afford Procore

**Decision Criteria**:
- Price (must be <$500/month total)
- Ease of setup (<1 week to deploy)
- Mobile access (field workers need phones)
- Training time (<2 hours per user)
- Support quality (need handholding)

### Secondary: "Growing Gary"
**Profile**:
- Smaller contractor growing fast
- $2M-$5M revenue, 5-15 employees
- Currently using QuickBooks + spreadsheets
- Tech-savvy, early adopter

**Buying Triggers**:
- Won a big project, needs to scale operations
- Hired project manager, needs collaboration tools
- Bank/investor requesting better reporting
- Insurance requiring safety documentation

   ```
      - Live walk-through
      - Answer objections
   5. Proposal (same day):
      - Custom pricing if needed
      - Implementation plan
   6. Follow-up (next day):
      - Address concerns
      - Close or schedule next call
   7. Onboarding (week 1):
      - Setup call
      - Data import
      - Training
   ```

3. **Customer Success** (Reduce Churn)
   - Weekly check-ins (first month)

   - Monitor usage (alert if drops)
